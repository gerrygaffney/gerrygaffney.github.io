Dubbo courthouse

Brisbane St
Dubbo NSW


Transport

On-street parking is restricted to 1 hour in most places. Nearest free on-street parking is Bligh St.

The courthouse is 5 minutes walk from Dubbo railway station.

Accessibility

Dubbo railway station has good accessible access.

The courthouse is fully accessible.

More information

The courthouse can be busy. When you arrive, follow the "Juror" signs to the juror assembly area.

When arriving for jury duty, allow a full day (until 5pm), although you may be released much earlier.

There are plenty of nearby cafes, but you can bring your own snacks if you prefer. If you are chosen as a juror, your meals will be provided.

Smoking is not permitted in the courthouse (including the courthouse steps). There is a smokers' area just outside the courthous.
