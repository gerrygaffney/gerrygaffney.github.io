You have been included on a Jury Roll

You have been included on the jury roll for the district of Bourke.

The roll runs for one year, from April 1 2014 to March 31 2015.

During that time, you may be called (summoned) to serve on a jury.

What you need to do

You do not have to take any action now. We recommend that you read the brochure attached to this letter, which contains answers to common questions.

If you have holidays, exams or other commitments

You can let us know about dates that would make it difficult for you to serve, or by calling 1300 JUROR.

If you are unable to serve

Jury service is a rewarding responsibility, and is a fundamental part of our justice system.

Employers are obliged to release staff for jury service. Jurors will receive payment for their service.

However, if jury service will cause undue hardship, or for people in certain jobs, you can request to be removed from the jury roll.

If your personal details have changed

If your name or address have changed, let us know.
