Inconvenient dates

YWe understand that jury service can be inconvenient. You can nominate dates that are difficult for you (for example, because of travel), and we will try to avoid them.

Can I be removed from the Jury Roll?

People in certain roles (law enforcement, doctors, clergy), or with who are unable to perform their duty because of disability, may be exempt or ineligible from jury duty. You can apply online to be removed from the roll.

Jury service - a rewarding responsibility

Jury service plays a central role in our justice system. It allows members of our community to play an active role in the administration of justice.
